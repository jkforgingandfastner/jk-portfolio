Guide Clip

Gun Type Thimble

Strong gun type thimbles for wire rope assemblies, reinforcing loops and preventing deformation under load in industrial rigging applications.

Enquiry Now

OTIS Type Thimble

Premium OTIS type thimbles for elevator rope fittings, providing maximum strength, safety, and durability for critical lifting and hoisting applications.

Enquiry Now

Pan Thimble

Stainless steel pan thimbles protect wire rope eyes from abrasion, ensuring longevity and reliable performance in marine and rigging uses.
