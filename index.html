About Us

  • 2017

    Our Humble Beginnings

    JK Forging and Fastener began its journey in 2017 as a small unit with a strong vision to deliver quality forging and fastener products to local industries.

  • 2018

    Building Strong Foundations

    With continuous dedication, we upgraded our machinery and processes to manufacture reliable anchor fasteners, bolts, thimbles, and clips for diverse applications.

  • 2020

    Expanding Capabilities

    Expanded operations with advanced production lines and skilled workforce to cater to construction, mechanical, and automotive industry requirements efficiently.

  • 2023

    Towards Global Reach

    Focused on enhancing product quality standards, increasing production capacity, and building relationships with national and international clients for long-term growth.
